Which areas do you cover?
We work in a 40 mile radius of Bournemouth where we are based. So Dorset, Hampshire, and up to Yeovil and Salisbury.
Who are your clients?
Our clients fall into the following categories – Home Owners, Landlords, Property Management Companies, Housing Associations, Estate Agents, Solicitors, Local Authorities, and Builders.
What do you move for them?
We move furniture and appliances, full house clearances, general waste, garden waste and commercial waste.
